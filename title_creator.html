Notes: The company brand standards require site headers to be .jpg or .png files using a designated font with four title styles available.

Titles were created using Adobe products (PhotoShop or Fireworks) with very specific kerning and leading applied based on the title style.

With the advent of Adobe moving products to the cloud the company ceased providing licenses and installation of Adobe products.

I created this html page for our developers to create their site titles with the absence of image editing products.

The CSS is built specifically to provide the correct kerning and leading for each title style.
